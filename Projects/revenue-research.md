## Market Reality Check (March 2026)

Buyers are not paying for "AI consulting." They pay for **solved problems with clear ROI**. Top 5 services with confirmed paying customers right now:

1. **AI chatbot / voice agent for local SMBs** — Lead capture, booking, FAQ. ~62% of small businesses still take calls manually. Price: $300–$1,500 setup + $150–$500/month.
2. **Workflow automation (n8n / Make.com)** — CRM connections, forms, contracts, notifications. $300–$2,000/month retainer.
3. **AI-powered outreach / lead gen systems** — Email/LinkedIn sequences with AI personalization. $1,000–$3,000/month per brand.
4. **Custom AI agent development (Upwork/direct)** — RAG, LLM integrations, autonomous pipelines. $150–$300/hr senior rate.
5. **Micro-SaaS AI tools** — $49–$99/month, targeting a repeatable professional workflow. Timeline: 4–8 weeks to first paying customer.

## Underserved Niches With Confirmed Buyers

1. **Tradespeople (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, Poland/EU)** — No-show prevention, automated reminders, quote follow-up. They lose real money and know it.
2. **Solo lawyers / accountants** — Client intake automation, document prep. Low competition in Polish/EU market.
3. **Fitness studios / wellness clinics** — Booking + follow-up. $400/month for automation is a proven sale.
4. **Real estate agents** — Lead follow-up automation. High LTV, perpetually understaffed.
5. **E-commerce SMBs** — Post-purchase flows, review solicitation, abandoned cart agents.
