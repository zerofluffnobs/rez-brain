# Day-1 Launch Checklist

---

**What you need:**
- [ ] A dedicated email address (proton.me recommended)
- [ ] 60–90 minutes of focus time
- [ ] Your GitHub login (zerofluffnobs)
- [ ] A profile photo (or skip and add later — don't let it block you)

## PHASE 6: First Cold Outreach (10 min)

> Templates are in: `Projects/outreach-templates.md`

Pick ONE niche for your first 5 emails. Recommended: **tradespeople** (HVAC/plumbing/electrical) — highest pain, lowest sophistication, most likely to respond.

- [ ] Find 5 local tradespeople on Google Maps (your city or nearby)
  - Look for: no online booking, no chatbot, basic website, 10–50 reviews
  - Get their contact email from the website
- [ ] Send Template CE-1 (from outreach-templates.md) to all 5 — personalise [Name] and [City] only
- [ ] Log them in a simple spreadsheet: Name | Business | Date Sent | Response

> **Goal this week:** 5 emails out = 1 reply = 1 discovery call booked. Not more, not less.

---

## PHASE 7: Set Up Tracking (5 min)

This keeps ADHD-brain from losing track of what's happening.

- [ ] Create a simple spreadsheet (Google Sheets or Notion):
  - Sheet 1: **Outreach** — Name, Niche, Platform, Date, Status (Sent / Replied / Call Booked / Closed)
  - Sheet 2: **Clients** — Name, Service, Start Date, End Date, Amount, Status
  - Sheet 3: **Monthly Revenue** — Month, Target, Actual
- [ ] First month target: **€400** (1 basic chatbot setup)
- [ ] Second month target: **€800** (2 projects or 1 retainer)
