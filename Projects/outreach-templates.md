## Section 5: Qualifying Question Sequence

When a prospect responds, use this sequence to qualify before quoting:

**Round 1 (before any call):**
1. "What does your current process look like for [their pain point]?"
2. "How many [inquiries/bookings/leads] are you handling per week?"
3. "Is there a specific tool you're already using that this needs to connect to?"

**Round 2 (on call):**
4. "What's the worst version of this problem — what did it cost you recently?"
5. "Who else is involved in this decision?"
6. "What would success look like in 90 days?"
7. "What's your timeline for getting this in place?"

**Disqualify if:**
- They want free trials with no budget conversation
- They've "tried 3 other developers and it didn't work" (dig into why)
- No clear business process to automate — pure exploration only
- Timeline is "no rush" with no urgency signal

**Quote trigger:** If they have a clear problem, a stated or implied budget, a timeline, and a decision-maker on the call — send a proposal within 24 hours.
