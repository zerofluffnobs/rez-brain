## Package 1: AI Chatbot for Local Businesses

### Headline (Contra/Upwork)
**AI Chatbot That Captures Leads & Books Appointments 24/7 — No Coding Needed on Your Side**

### Headline (Fiverr)
**I will build an AI chatbot for your local business that captures leads and books appointments**

### Description
You're losing customers every time your phone goes unanswered or your website sits silent after hours. I build AI chatbots that greet visitors, answer FAQs, capture lead information, and book appointments — automatically, around the clock.

These aren't generic chatbot templates. Each one is trained on your business: your services, your prices, your FAQs, your tone. When a customer asks "Do you work on weekends?" or "How much does it cost?", they get a real answer, not a runaround.

**What you get:**
- Fully custom AI chatbot trained on your business info
- Website embed (works on any site — WordPress, Squarespace, custom)
- Lead capture with email/SMS notification to you
- Appointment booking integration (Calendly, Cal.com, or custom)
- 7-day testing period with tweaks included
- Handoff guide so you understand what you own

**Best for:** Clinics, dental offices, law firms, fitness studios, tradespeople (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), real estate agents, restaurants.

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $400 setup + $150/month | Basic FAQ + lead capture, web embed, email notifications |
| **Pro** | $750 setup + $250/month | Full chatbot + booking integration + CRM connection + SMS alerts |
| **Business** | $1,200 setup + $400/month | Multi-location, voice agent option, custom integrations, priority support |

*Monthly fee covers: hosting, AI API costs, maintenance, 1 update/month.*

### FAQ

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
A: No. You give me your business info (services, prices, FAQs), I build it, you get a one-line embed code to paste into your website. That's it.

**Q: What if I don't have a website?**
A: I can host the chatbot as a standalone landing page or integrate it with a booking link you share directly.

**Q: Will it really answer questions correctly?**
A: It answers based on what you tell me. I work with you during setup to review sample questions and tune the responses. You test it before it goes live.

**Q: Can it replace my receptionist?**
A: It handles the routine 80%: FAQs, lead capture, scheduling. It escalates anything complex to you. Many clients see a 40–60% reduction in phone interruptions.

**Q: What AI does it use?**
A: Claude (Anthropic) or GPT-4o — your choice. Both are enterprise-grade, GDPR-friendly options.

**Q: Can I cancel the monthly plan?**
A: Yes, 30 days notice. You keep the chatbot code; the AI API subscription moves to your name.

## Package 2: Workflow Automation (n8n / Make.com)

### Headline (Contra/Upwork)
**Automate Your Business Workflows With n8n or Make — No More Manual Copy-Paste**

### Headline (Fiverr)
**I will automate your business workflows using n8n or Make.com**

### Description
Every hour your team spends copying data between apps, sending manual follow-up emails, or updating spreadsheets is an hour you're paying for work a machine should do.

I build workflow automations that connect your existing tools — CRM, email, calendar, invoicing, project management — and make them work together automatically. You describe the process, I automate it.

**Common automations I build:**
- New lead in form → CRM entry → welcome email → owner notification
- Appointment booked → confirmation SMS → reminder 24 hours before → post-appointment review request
- Invoice sent → payment received → receipt auto-sent → spreadsheet updated → Slack notification
- New Upwork/job post matching your criteria → daily digest email
- Customer support ticket → AI categorization → routed to right team member

**What you get:**
- End-to-end workflow automation (up to 5 nodes/steps per flow in Starter)
- Full documentation of what was built and why
- Tested + live before handoff
- 14-day support window post-delivery

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **Single Flow** | $300–$600 one-time | One workflow, up to 8 steps, 2 apps connected |
| **Business Bundle** | $800–$1,500 one-time | 3–5 workflows, up to 5 apps, full documentation |
| **Retainer** | $500–$1,200/month | Ongoing automation: new flows, maintenance, monitoring, priority response |

*Project price depends on complexity. I provide exact quote after 20-min scoping call.*

### FAQ

**Q: What tools do you connect?**
A: Any app with an API or that n8n/Make supports — which is 400+ tools: Gmail, Outlook, Slack, HubSpot, Notion, Airtable, Stripe, Calendly, WhatsApp, Shopify, WooCommerce, and more.

**Q: Do I need to host anything?**
A: I can run it on your existing n8n/Make account or set one up for you. Cloud options start at $20/month (Make) or self-hosted for near-zero cost.

**Q: Will this break if one of my apps updates?**
A: Webhook-based flows are resilient. API-based flows occasionally need a tweak when apps update. That's what the retainer is for, or I document what to watch.

**Q: How do I describe what I want automated?**
A: Walk me through it like you'd explain it to a new employee: "When X happens, I do Y, then Z." That's enough. I handle the technical translation.

**Q: What if I want to change it later?**
A: Single-flow clients get 14 days of changes. After that, changes are billed at $75/hour or covered by retainer.

## Package 3: Custom AI Agent Development

### Headline (Contra/Upwork)
**Custom AI Agent Development — RAG, LLM Pipelines, Autonomous Workflows in Python**

### Headline (Fiverr)
**I will build a custom AI agent, RAG pipeline, or LLM integration for your application**

### Description
You have a use case that generic AI products don't cover. You need something purpose-built: an agent that reads your documents, an LLM pipeline wired into your backend, an autonomous workflow that makes decisions and takes actions.

I build production-ready AI agents and integrations using Python, FastAPI, LangChain/LangGraph, and the Claude or OpenAI APIs. I've designed multi-agent dispatch/harvest architectures, built RAG systems over custom knowledge bases, and shipped containerized AI microservices.

**What I build:**
- RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems — chat with your documents, knowledge base, or database
- Autonomous AI agents — task decomposition, tool use, decision loops
- LLM integrations into existing backends (REST API wrappers, webhook handlers)
- Multi-agent pipelines — orchestrator + specialist worker containers
- AI-powered data extraction and transformation pipelines
- Custom chatbots with memory, context management, and structured outputs

**Stack:** Python 3.11+, FastAPI, Docker, PostgreSQL/pgvector, Redis, LangChain/LangGraph, Claude API, OpenAI API, n8n for orchestration where appropriate.

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **Integration** | $500–$1,200 | Single LLM integration: one endpoint, structured I/O, deployed + documented |
| **Agent Build** | $1,500–$3,500 | Full autonomous agent: tools, memory, state management, Docker container |
| **Pipeline** | $3,000–$8,000+ | Multi-agent system: orchestrator + workers, persistence, monitoring, handoff |
| **Hourly** | $120–$180/hr | For well-scoped additions, debugging, or architecture consulting |

*All project quotes require a scoping call. Milestone billing available on projects >$1,500.*

### FAQ

**Q: What's your typical delivery timeline?**
A: Single integration: 3–7 days. Full agent: 1–3 weeks. Pipeline: 3–6 weeks. Depends on requirements stability — clear spec = fast delivery.

**Q: Can you work with our existing codebase?**
A: Yes. I prefer to review existing code before quoting so I'm not guessing about integration complexity.

**Q: Do you write tests?**
A: Yes. Unit tests for core logic, integration tests for LLM behavior, and load tests for anything customer-facing. Test coverage spec agreed upfront.

**Q: Which LLM provider should I use?**
A: Depends on your use case and constraints. Claude (Anthropic) for nuanced reasoning and long-context tasks. GPT-4o for broad tool ecosystem. Local models (Ollama/Mistral) if data privacy is non-negotiable. I can advise after understanding your requirements.

**Q: Do you handle deployment?**
A: Yes — Docker containers, AWS/GCP/fly.io. I can deploy to your infra or spin up a minimal cloud setup. Ongoing DevOps support available.

**Q: Can you sign an NDA?**
A: Yes, standard NDA available, or I'll sign yours. Common for enterprise work.

**Q: What makes you different from other AI developers on Upwork?**
A: I design systems that survive past the demo: proper error handling, logging, state persistence, and documentation so your team can maintain what I build. I've designed full multi-agent dispatch architectures, not just "call the OpenAI API and return a string."
