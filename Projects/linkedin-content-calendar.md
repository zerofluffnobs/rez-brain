# LinkedIn Content Calendar — 2 Weeks

## Strategy

**Goal:** Be recognized as "the AI automation person for small businesses" before we hit DMs.
**Cadence:** 1 post/day, Mon–Fri (weekends off — SMB audience isn't on LinkedIn then).
**Tone:** Practical. No hype. Real numbers. SMB-owner language.
**Mix:** 40% repurposed blog content, 30% quick tips, 20% case study teasers, 10% personal/POV.

Post format that performs:
- Hook line (1–2 sentences, no "I'm thrilled to announce")
- Body (3–6 short paragraphs or bullet list)
- CTA (comment, DM, or link — never all three)
- 3–5 hashtags max, appended at the bottom

### Day 5 (Fri) — Quick Tip
**Type:** Quick tip
**Title:** The 3-question test for "should I automate this?"

> Before spending a cent on automation, ask:
>
> 1. Does this happen more than 3x/week?
> 2. Is the process the same every time (or close)?
> 3. Does someone dread doing it?
>
> If yes to all three — automate it.
>
> If no to any — wait. You don't have a systems problem yet.
>
> Most SMBs have 3–5 tasks that pass this test immediately.
>
> Want to know what yours are? Comment your industry below.
